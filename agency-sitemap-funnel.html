Site Map & Client Acquisition Funnel

A premium positioning strategy for entering the competitive tender landscape — without ever using the word "tender."

01 · Foundation

Strategic Positioning Principles

I

Authority Over Availability

Never position as "available for projects." Instead, signal that you selectively engage with clients whose ambitions match your standards. Scarcity and discernment build premium perception.

II

Outcomes, Not Outputs

Clients commissioning large-scale work buy results — revenue, reputation, market position. Every page, case study, and CTA should speak to transformation, not deliverables.

III

Invisible Process, Visible Proof

The mechanics of how you win work (competitive bids, submissions) stay behind the curtain. What's visible is your track record, methodology, and the calibre of organisations you've elevated.

02 · Architecture

Site Map

Home
/
Awareness

Purpose

Immediate authority signal. Communicate who you are for, not what you do. Filter out the wrong clients in the first 5 seconds.

Key Sections

  • Hero: Bold positioning statement
  • Selected work (3 hero case studies)
  • Credibility strip (client logos/awards)
  • Manifesto teaser → About

Conversion Goal

Drive to Work or About. Seed curiosity. No aggressive CTA — premium brands invite, not push.

Work
/work
Trust

Purpose

The primary trust engine. Showcases the depth, scale, and impact of your engagements. This is where large organisations self-qualify.

Key Sections

  • Filterable by sector / scale
  • Deep case studies with results
  • Before/after narrative structure
  • "Organisations like yours" framing

Conversion Goal

Prospect reads one full case study and sees themselves in it. Natural next step: Enquire.

Expertise
/expertise
Trust

Purpose

Replace a "Services" page with a demonstration of mastery. Positions you as a strategic partner, not a vendor. Critical for large-scale client confidence.

Key Sections

  • Discipline areas (Brand, Digital, etc.)
  • Methodology / how you think
  • Strategic process overview
  • Sectors you work within

Conversion Goal

Validate capability for prospect's brief. Removes procurement objections before the first conversation.

Studio
/studio
Trust

Purpose

Humanise the agency. Large organisations and procurement teams need to trust the people, not just the portfolio. This page is about values, leadership, and longevity.

Key Sections

  • Founding story / manifesto
  • Leadership team profiles
  • Studio values and approach
  • Accolades, press, recognition

Conversion Goal

Build personal connection with decision-makers. Supports formal qualification processes.

Perspectives
/perspectives
Awareness

Purpose

Thought leadership that attracts your ideal client via SEO and sharing. Demonstrates intellectual depth. Replaces a generic "Blog."

Key Sections

  • Long-form essays on design & business
  • Sector-specific insights
  • Project reflections / behind the brief
  • Annual trends or reports

Begin
/begin
Convert

The enquiry page — but framed as starting a relationship, not submitting a form. The language and design must feel as considered as the rest of the site.

Discovery & Attraction
Stage 01 · Top of Funnel

Channels

  • Organic search (sector-specific keywords)
  • Design press & award listings
  • LinkedIn thought leadership
  • Referrals from existing clients
  • Speaking engagements

Site Pages Activated

  • Home — immediate authority signal
  • Perspectives — SEO & shareability
  • Award / press listings link back
7s
Average time to form a first impression. Your home page must communicate premium positioning and relevance within one scroll.
Consideration & Research
Stage 02 · Mid Funnel

Prospect Behaviour

  • Deep dive into case studies
  • Reading your Perspectives content
  • Looking up team on LinkedIn
  • Checking client roster credibility
  • Sharing your site internally

Site Pages Activated

  • Work — case study depth & proof
  • Expertise — methodology confidence
  • Studio — team credibility
3–5
Pages a serious prospect will visit before making contact. Every page must sustain and deepen trust without friction.
Validation & Trust
Stage 03 · Intent Signal

What Validates You

  • Recognisable client names & sectors
  • Quantified outcomes in case studies
  • Testimonials from senior stakeholders
  • Awards & industry recognition
  • Years of operation & team depth

Procurement-Specific Trust Signals

  • Company registration & credentials
  • Insurance & compliance indicators
  • Transparent team size & capacity
  • Clear engagement process
68%
Of B2B buyers complete the majority of their research before making first contact. Your site must close objections before the conversation begins.
Engagement & Qualification
Stage 04 · Conversion

Enquiry Experience

  • Begin page: warm, considered copy
  • Brief qualifier questions (4–6 max)
  • Budget range selector (no awkwardness)
  • Immediate auto-reply with next steps
  • Personal response within 24 hours

Qualification Criteria

  • Project scale & ambition fit
  • Timeline is realistic
  • Decision-maker is in contact
  • Budget signals genuine investment
24h
Maximum response time to an enquiry. Premium agencies respond fast and personally — this itself communicates the quality of service to come.
Proposal & Commission
Stage 05 · Close

The Credentials Meeting

  • Bespoke presentation — never generic
  • Deep knowledge of their sector
  • Reference relevant case studies only
  • Lead with questions, not features
  • Confirm you understand their ambition

Proposal Best Practice

  • Strategic thinking before creative
  • Outcome-focused scope language
  • Investment framing (not cost)
  • Clear phasing and milestones
  • Single point of contact named
1:3
Target win ratio for competitive engagements when your positioning is tight. If you're losing more than this, the issue is usually misaligned qualification, not creative quality.
04 · Voice

Brand Language Guide

❌ Avoid — Commodity Language

Tendering / BidsWe participate in competitive procurement processes.
Affordable / Competitive ratesSignals price competition, not premium value.
Full service agencyVague, forgettable, undifferentiated.
We'd love to work with youDesperate. Premium brands choose relationships.

✓ Use — Authority Language

Commissioned Work / Selected EngagementsPositions each project as curated and intentional.
Investment / PartnershipReframes cost as mutual commitment to outcomes.
Strategic design studioSpecific, elevated, memorable.
We work with organisations who…Selective framing that attracts the right clients.
