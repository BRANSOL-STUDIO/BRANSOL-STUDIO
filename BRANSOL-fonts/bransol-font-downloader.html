BRANSOL

Font Downloader

Downloads all 21 font files used across the BRANSOL platform and saves them to your device. Once complete, place the folders next to bransol-fonts.css and uncomment the self-hosted section.

After downloading:
1. All .woff2 files will save to your Downloads folder
2. Create subfolders: syne/ dm-sans/ dm-mono/ cormorant/ outfit/ instrument-serif/
3. Move each file into the correct subfolder
4. Open bransol-fonts.css and uncomment the self-hosted section
5. Remove the @import lines at the top

Folder structure:
BRANSOL-fonts/
├── bransol-fonts.css
├── syne/ Syne-400 500 600 700 800.woff2
├── dm-sans/ DMSans-300 400 400i 500.woff2
├── dm-mono/ DMMono-300 400.woff2
├── cormorant/ CormorantGaramond-300 300i 400 400i 600.woff2
├── outfit/ Outfit-200 300 400 500.woff2
└── instrument-serif/ InstrumentSerif-Regular Italic.woff2
